Frequently Asked Questions

Types of Rooms?

At EDUFY, we offer two primary types of study rooms: Public Rooms and Private Rooms. Public Rooms are open to all users and ideal for collaborative learning, community discussions, or open-topic study sessions. Anyone can request to join, and these rooms foster knowledge sharing across a wider audience. They’re perfect for students preparing for competitive exams, subject-specific groups, or even peer mentoring.


Private Rooms, on the other hand, are invite-only or require approval to join. These are designed for focused group work, such as project teams, tutoring sessions, or study circles. Hosts have full control over participation, content, and room management. With features like file uploads, curriculum organization, and participant moderation, EDUFY rooms provide a dynamic, structured, and user-friendly environment for every learner’s need.

How Auctions Work?

At EDUFY, auctions provide a dynamic way for users to access premium resources, tutoring sessions, or exclusive study materials. When an auction is listed, users can place bids within a set timeframe. The highest bidder at the end of the auction wins the offering—whether it’s a one-on-one session with a tutor, a curriculum package, or unique academic content. All bids are visible in real-time, making the process transparent and engaging.


To participate, users simply need to be registered and logged into their EDUFY account. Notifications are sent for key updates like being outbid or winning an auction. EDUFY ensures secure bidding and fair competition. Whether you're looking to enhance your learning or monetize your expertise, the auction system opens new possibilities for both learners and educators on the platform.

What is EDUFY and how does it help students?

EDUFY is a collaborative online learning platform that allows students to create, join, and manage virtual study rooms. These rooms serve as focused spaces where learners can share knowledge, discuss topics, upload study materials, and engage in academic conversation. Whether you're studying for exams, working on group projects, or exploring a subject in-depth, EDUFY provides an interactive environment designed to boost productivity and enhance understanding.


The platform empowers students to take charge of their learning by offering tools such as file sharing, discussion boards, and real-time messaging. Hosts can moderate discussions, manage participants, and ensure the quality of content being shared. By bringing together students with similar academic goals, EDUFY turns solitary studying into a supportive and efficient group experience.

How do I join a study room?

Joining a room on EDUFY is simple and student-friendly. You can browse or search for rooms based on topic, subject, or creator. Once you find a room of interest, click the "Join" button. If the room is open, you’ll be added immediately. If it requires approval, a request will be sent to the host, who will then accept or reject your entry.


After being approved, you’ll get a notification confirming your access. You can then view room content, download shared files, participate in discussions, and collaborate with other participants. This controlled access system ensures rooms remain focused and relevant while giving hosts full authority over their space.

Can I share files or study materials in a room?

Yes, EDUFY encourages collaborative learning by enabling file sharing within each study room. Whether it's lecture notes, assignments, presentations, or PDFs, participants and hosts can upload materials directly to the room. These files are easily accessible to all room members, creating a shared knowledge base that enhances learning.


This feature is especially helpful for group study sessions or when preparing for exams. Instead of juggling emails or external drives, all your academic resources live in one place, organized by room and topic. The file-sharing capability keeps the learning environment centralized and clutter-free.

What control do hosts have in their rooms?

Room hosts on EDUFY have powerful tools to manage the learning environment. They can approve or reject join requests, remove participants, moderate discussions, and oversee file uploads. This control helps maintain a focused and respectful atmosphere, where participants can collaborate effectively.


In addition, hosts receive notifications about new join requests or any significant changes in the room. They can also upload key study materials and guide discussions toward productive outcomes. EDUFY ensures that hosts have the necessary authority to curate their rooms while promoting collaboration and peer learning.
